## 1. Scope
This Privacy Policy explains how **the bc2adls team** (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards personal data in connection with:

- The **bc2adls** app distributed via **Microsoft AppSource**;  
- Our websites and support channels used for documentation, issue reporting, or licensing;  
- Related communications with customers, administrators, and end users.

> **Note**: Microsoft services (e.g., Dynamics 365, Power Platform, Azure, Microsoft 365) are governed by Microsoft’s own privacy commitments.

---

## 2. Roles and Responsibilities
- For data processed inside your Microsoft tenant, your organization is the **data controller**. We act as a **data processor** for telemetry or support data.
- For websites and direct communications, we act as the **data controller**.

---

## 3. What We Collect
We minimize personal data collection:

**A. App telemetry and licensing**  
- App identifier, tenant/environment IDs, usage events, diagnostics, license state.
- We **do not** collect business content unless you share it for support.

**B. Support interactions**  
- Information you provide (emails, logs, screenshots). Please mask sensitive data.

**C. Website and device data**  
- IP address, user-agent, pages viewed, timestamps, cookies, analytics.

We do **not** collect biometric, health, financial, or children’s data.
